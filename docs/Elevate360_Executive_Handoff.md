## Safety Posture

### Keep approval required for:
- High-risk execution changes
- Broad offer or CTA changes
- Policy changes
- Safe mode / kill switch actions
- Major brand-wide growth actions

### Allow safe auto-apply only where:
- Confidence is high
- Risk is low
- Rollback is available
- Metrics are observable

### Always review after any rollout:
- Rollback counts
- Critical alerts
- Revenue truth changes
- Maturity score drops
- Failed job count increases
