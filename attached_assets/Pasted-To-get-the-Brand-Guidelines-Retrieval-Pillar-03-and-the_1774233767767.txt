To get the Brand Guidelines Retrieval (Pillar 03) and the Voice Engine (Pillar 01) running on Replit, follow these steps:

1. Set up your Secrets (Environment Variables)
In your Replit sidebar, click on Tools > Secrets and add the following keys. This keeps your credentials safe:

ANTHROPIC_API_KEY: From your Anthropic Console.

SUPABASE_URL: From your Supabase Project Settings.

SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY: From Supabase (needed for vector queries).
