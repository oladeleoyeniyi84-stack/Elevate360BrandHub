Using your verified creator photo as the face of the Elevate360 AI Concierge will make the platform feel more premium, personal, and brand-authentic. And making it “come to life” during the five session types is even stronger.

Best implementation approach

I recommend a 3-layer presence system:

1. Static branded presence

Use your verified creator photo in:

the floating chat launcher
the concierge header
the welcome message card
each consultation/session card

This replaces the generic concierge icon with your real creator identity.

2. Live conversational presence

When a user opens one of these:

Brand Strategy Session
AI Content Consultation
Creative Direction Call
App / Product Consultation
Collaboration Discovery Call

the concierge area should shift from static photo to a live presence state:

subtle breathing animation
soft glow ring
speaking pulse when AI responds
optional short idle motion loop

So it feels like your verified creator assistant is present, not just a chatbot.

3. Session-specific persona mode

Each session should have its own version of your concierge presence.

For example:

Brand Strategy Session
confident, executive, strategic tone
AI Content Consultation
creative, energetic, teaching tone
Creative Direction Call
premium visual consultant tone
App / Product Consultation
technical, product-focused tone
Collaboration Discovery Call
warm, partnership-driven tone

That means the same verified creator face, but with different:

title
intro line
prompt preset
CTA
visual accent text
